I've always been fascinated 🤩 by the design world since I was a kid.

I started by drawing illustrations throughout my childhood, studying visual communication design at the university, then working as a graphic designer ever since I graduated.

Seeing the world shifting to digital has made my interest expand to digital design and technology. I was given the opportunity to redesign the website of my previous company and update the internal web app of my current company, where I collaborated closely with the developer team and the stakeholders as users.

This is where I discovered my newfound passion: digital product design, which led to my decision to do a UX/UI design bootcamp, where I learned to approach a problem by assessing business viability, technical feasibility, and user desirability. My goal is to translate the business objectives into an optimal product that solves the user's problem while meeting the business and technical constraints. It's never a smooth process and involves numerous iterations, but it always brings me joy when everything falls into place.

My background in graphic design has given me a solid foundation in visual design. I also enjoy coding with HTML/CSS and aspire to have more front-end development skills under my belt. Apart from being well-organized and having excellent attention to detail, I am a firm believer in 'less is more'.
