Intercruises Shorex Categories

Campaign

As a reflection of the cruise industry evolution, Intercruises has recategorized its entire shore excursion portfolio into five main categories made up of 22 subcategories of fully inclusive experiences, suitable for all guest demographics. Each of the five main categories has a designated image and color. Twenty-two unique icons were made to represent the subcategories. As part of the social media campaign, each main category is presented by a short video.
