Dow Jones Interoperable Modules

B2B Modular Web App

Interoperable Modules are a modular solution that integrates Dow Jones' news and data directly into customers' workspaces to optimize their workflows.

To continue providing award-winning news and data where it is needed, Dow Jones developed pre-built news modules designed to integrate seamlessly into OpenFin, a unified workspace used by major financial firms. Featuring premium financial and business content from renowned Dow Jones brands such as Newswires, The Wall Street Journal, Barron's, MarketWatch and Investor's Business Daily, these news and data modules optimize users' workflows by streamlining access to the latest news, analysis, commentary and event data. The project was to create an MVP, starting with the most essential module ‚Äì Streaming News.

Design Process üë®üèª‚Äçüíª

We began by understanding the requirements, users' needs and technical constraints. These are the must-haves:

  • News headlines with timestamp
  • Category and time selection
  • News filters
  • Search bar ‚Äì to search for news
  • Ability to save searches and recall them
We conducted a competitive analysis, worked on the wireframes and then the visual design.

After the first version of Streaming News module was shipped, we gathered feedback and learned that it didn't work well for the users.

"I want to see more headlines on the screen, scrolling is wasting my time."

We realized that users typically had this module open in a small portion of the screen, combined with other modules they were using in their workspace. Therefore, we had to optimize the usage of screen real estate to improve data visibility.

In the following iteration, we made several improvements:

  • Reduced all elements' size and spacing while ensuring legibility
  • Rearranged the controls to optimize both screen usage and user flow
  • Added breakpoints for small and large screen size

We also added new features:

  • AND/OR boolean operator in the search bar for a more refined search
  • Dedicated page to manage saved searches and notifications
