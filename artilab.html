Artilab

Brand Identity

Artilab is a lab-consultancy focusing on creating sensorial experiences for cities and brands. It was born in 2019 in Barcelona. The name Artilab bears a philosophy. In Catalan, the letter 'i' means 'and'. In the logo, this letter is set slightly below the line, as if it's there to connect the two words, 'art' and 'lab'. On the other hand, 'arti' also means 'meaning' in Indonesian.

Artilab develops strategies through sensorial experiences approach, the five senses — sight, smell, taste, hearing and touch. Through these five senses, a tailored experience of a certain city can emerge.
