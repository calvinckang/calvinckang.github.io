Cena Solidaria de Navidad

Campaign

Cena Solidaria de Navidad is an annual Christmas charity dinner held by the Fundación Universitaria San Antonio. Portrayed as the visual of the campaign is the foundation's own nativity scene, which its creation has been a Christmas tradition in Spain for centuries.
